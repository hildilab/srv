Fast Search

Search the complete database by one or more characteristics.

* PDB ID Search MPPD by one or more PDB IDs separated by (","). example: 1ar1, 1xio
* Keywords Search MPPD by one or more PDB record KEYWDS. Partial matches are allowed. example: transporter
* Family Search MPPD by one specific family. Families are defined by the regularly updated database of Stephen White. For channels and transporters the classification of the Milton Saier laboratory is used. For all other entries indexing of the primary reference is employed. example: antiporters


Advanced Search
* Experimental Choose a method(s) of protein structure determination example: X-Ray
Choose the range of structure resolution. example: 0 to 3.5 Å
* Families The database can be searched by families defined by the regularly updated database of Stephen White. For channels and transporters the classification of the Milton Saier laboratory is accordingly used. For all other entries indexing of the primary reference is employed.
Here you can compose a set of different families i.e. all channels.


Results

 Results are provided in form of a tabular overview. In the first column a link to Provi "View", a download link "download" and a link to the RCSB-PDB "PDB" is provided.
